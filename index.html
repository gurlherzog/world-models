A Possible Genealogy of World Models

Filterable by ontological mode — ten entries, growing

This is an attempt to create a genealogy and taxonomy of world models. It is a work in progress. Each entry is a specific, bounded world model — not a tradition or a period, but a nameable system — examined through seven parameters: its technologies of inscription, the political and economic interests that sustained it, its use case and application, its aesthetics, the companion model of the human it implies, its transparency or opacity, and its cosmological register.
